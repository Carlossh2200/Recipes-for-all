Pizza Recipe

Making pizza is a fun activity to do when your'e hungry, just follow these easy steps!

  • Ingredients
    • Bread flour
    • Yeast
    • Sugar
    • Salt
    • Water
    • Olive oil
    • Tomato sauce
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Pepperoni
    • Onion
    • Green bell pepper
    • Garlic
    • Italian seasoning
  • Recipe
    1. Mix the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl
    2. Add the water and olive oil to the bowl
    3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
    4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour
    5. Roll out the dough into a circle
    6. Add the tomato sauce, cheese, pepperoni, onion, green bell pepper, garlic, and Italian seasoning to the dough
    7. Bake the pizza for 15 minutes at 450 degrees
