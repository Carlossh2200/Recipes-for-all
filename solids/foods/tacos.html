Carnitas tacos recipe

Making tacos is an easy task! heres how you can make everyones favorite dish in just these easy steps.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 lb of pork meat
    • 1 onion
    • 1 garlic clove
    • 1/2 cup of orange juice
    • 1/2 cup of water
    • 1/2 cup of milk
    • 1/2 cup of oil
    • 1/2 cup of cilantro
    • 1/2 cup of onion
    • 1/2 cup of salsa
    • 1/2 cup of lime juice
    • 1/2 cup of salt
    • 1/2 cup of pepper
    • 1/2 cup of tortillas
  • Instructions:
    • 1. Cut the pork meat into small pieces
    • 2. Cut the onion and garlic into small pieces
    • 3. In a pan, add the oil and heat it up
    • 4. Add the pork meat, onion, and garlic to the pan
    • 5. Add the orange juice, water, milk, cilantro, onion, salsa, lime juice, salt, and pepper to the pan
    • 6. Cook for 30 minutes
    • 7. Serve the meat on the tortillas
