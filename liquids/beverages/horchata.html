Making this delicious beverage can be as easy as these next steps!

  • Ingredients
    • Milk
    • Rice
    • Vanilla
    • Cinnamon
  • Recipe
    1. Put the milk in a jar
    2. Blend rice
    3. Add the rice to the milk
    4. Add cinnamon
    5. Add vanilla drops
    6. Blend everything
    7. Serve cold and enjoy!
